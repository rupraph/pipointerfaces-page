Bluetooth midi

Prototype

The modules are still under development. Hence these directions are only valid for protytpe testing. Note: some features are not yet implemented, and some are not mature yet.

Any feedback is welcome !

To connect Pipo using BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy):

  • on a smartphone: the connection is usually executed directly in the music or synth app (BLE will not show up in the normal Bluetooth menu of your phone)
  • on a Mac: go to Audio Midi Setup app. Then in Menu->Window, make sure to show the MIDI Studio window. In the MIDI studio, there is a Bluetooth icon on the top right. Click on it. Pipo should be listed for connection. When connected, pipo should appear as a normal Midi peripheral in you other softwares.

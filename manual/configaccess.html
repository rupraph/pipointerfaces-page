Connect Pipo to your local wifi

This step is usefull either to access the config page, or to use OSC. Note: the USB and BLE midi wil work without wifi being connected

  • When you switch on a Pipo, it will always try to connect to its last working Wifi.

    • If it cannot connect, Pipo will start a WiFi Access Point called "Pipo" for you to connect to it.
      • In this case, use your phone or computer to conect on this Wifi
      • when you are connected to Pipo, your phone or pc should directly open your browser and bring you to the Pipo wifi manager page (if the window does not open, just open your webbrowser and go to 192.168.4.1)
      • You should now see the Wifi Manager page.
      • click configure wifi
      • select the wifi you want pipo to connect to
      • enter your credentials
      • click save
      • if succesfull, Pipo will restart, and after a few seconds, the Wifi Led on the pipo should start slowly pulsing. This means pipo is connected and running
    • If pipo can connect to previous wifi, it will do automatically

  • Now that Pipo is connected to your wifi, you can access the config page. Depending on your pipo, you can access it at:

    • pipo-motion.local
    • pipo-analog.local
    • pipo-range.local

  • From there you can configure your Pipo Freely:

    • The sensor section allow you to choose the measuring range, in the sensor unit.
    • The output section allow you to customize how your translate and scale the selected range to the verious possible outputs.

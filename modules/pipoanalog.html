Pipo Analog

Pipo Analog has:

  • 6 analog inputs

  • with input voltage range selection (3.3, 5 or 12v range)

  • with overvoltage and polarity inversion protection

  • 6 capacitive touch inputs

  • can be used as on/off touch input or proximity sensing

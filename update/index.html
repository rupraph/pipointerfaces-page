Update Pipo

How to update Pipo ?

  • Make sure you can access the reset and boot buttons at the back of the Pipo board (if in a casing, you have to remove it)
  • Connect Pipo to your computer
  • Put the Pipo into upload mode:
    • Maintain pushed the boot button (the small yellow button labeled "BO" on the board rev1.0)
    • Click the reset button once
    • Release the boot button
  • Click on a version to upload it to your Pipo

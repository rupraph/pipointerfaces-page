    What is the pipo project ?

    The Pipo project focuses on developping an ecosystem of MiDi/OSC control modules for creative people to easily build new interactions with computers, instruments or machines, focusing on sensing and intuitive use.

    • Inspire people to explore news ways to interact with creative media like sound, light, games, etc..

    • Lowers the technical barrier between creative work and electronics

    • Provides reliable and practical tools for custom systems.

    Who is pipo for ?

    Pipo is primarily designed as a tool to be incorporated in a wide variety of creative and experimental practices.

    Creative people

    Makers, Installations designers, Developpers, DIY people,...

    Artists

    Musicians, Plastic artists, Performers,...

    Performing arts professions

    Lighting engineers, stage director, dancers,...

    Education

    Teachers, students,...

    Modules

      pipo motion

      A 6 axis (orientation and acceleration) high precision wireless mouvement controller. Precise and sensitive motion for MiDi, OSC, or HID (Mouse/Gamepad mode)

        pipo analog

        6 voltage inputs to acquire any analog sensor (up to 12v) + 6 touch inputs as MiDi/OSC/HID

          pipo range

          A distance ranging controller. Sense your environement (up to 5m)

            User friendly config page

              Each modules hosts a WiFi configuration page to customize the output to your needs

    What is the pipo project ?

    The Pipo project focuses on developping an ecosystem of control modules for creative people to easily build new interactions with computers, instruments or machines, focusing on sensing and intuitive use.

    • Inspire people to explore news ways to interact with creative media like sound, light, games, etc..

    • Lowers the technical barrier between creative work and electronics

    • Provides tools for reliable custom systems quickly

    Who is pipo for ?

    Pipo is primarily designed as a tool to be incorporated in a wide variety of creative and experimental practices.

    Creative people

    Makers, Installations designers, Developpers, DIY people,...

    Artists

    Musicians, Plastic artists, Performers,...

    Performing arts professions

    Lighting engineers, stage director, dancers,...

    Education

    Teachers, students,...

    Main features

    MIDI

    OSC (OpenSoundControl)

    Bluetooth

    WiFi

    Gamepad mode

    USB-C

    Modules

      pipo motion

      A 6 axis high precision mouvement controller

        pipo analog

        6 voltage inputs for the analog sensor of your choice up to 12v) + 6 touch inputs

          pipo range

          A distance ranging controller

            User friendly config page

              Each modules hosts a WiFi configuration page to customize the output to your needs
